A lot of bunny coloring pages online are packed with fur texture and shading detail that's really aimed at older kids or adults. This one keeps it simple — big, clear shapes that are satisfying to fill in even for a 3-year-old. It works year-round, not just at Easter.

Classic white and pink is always a winner, but encourage kids to invent their own bunny colors. A blue bunny or a rainbow bunny is just as valid.
