Most Easter Bunny coloring pages feature elaborate baskets, eggs, flowers, and background details that overwhelm young kids. This version focuses on the bunny itself — a friendly face and simple body that's easy to complete in one sitting. Good for ages 3 and up.

It's a solid choice for Easter baskets, classroom parties, or just keeping kids busy on a spring afternoon. Pastel colors feel traditionally Easter-y, but bold crayon colors work just as well.
