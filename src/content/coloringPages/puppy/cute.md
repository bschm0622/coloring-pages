This cute puppy coloring page keeps things sweet and simple — big round eyes, soft ears, and a face that even the youngest kids can color without feeling overwhelmed. No fussy fur details, no tiny spaces to fill in. Just a puppy that's genuinely fun to bring to life with crayons or markers.

Any color works. Classic golden, chocolate brown, spotty black and white — or let them invent their own completely made-up puppy color.
