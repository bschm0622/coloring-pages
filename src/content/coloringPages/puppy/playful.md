This puppy is mid-play — tail up, ears perked, ready to pounce on something. It's the same simple line style as the other puppy pages but with more movement in the pose, which some kids find more interesting to color than a puppy just sitting there.

Still very manageable for young kids — the shapes are clean and the spaces are large enough for crayons. Any dog color works, and the playful pose looks great in bolder colors.
