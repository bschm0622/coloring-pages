This baby lamb coloring page is designed with young children in mind — the outline is clean and roomy, without the tiny fiddly sections that make so many coloring pages frustrating for little hands. Kids ages 2–6 will find it easy to stay inside the lines and feel proud of the result.

It makes a great spring or Easter activity. Try soft whites and creamy yellows for the wool, or let kids go wild with whatever colors they love — there are no rules.
