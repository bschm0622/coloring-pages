T-Rex coloring pages are often covered in scales, spikes, and background scenery that makes them look impressive but nearly impossible for young kids to actually color. This one strips it back to the essentials — a recognizable T-Rex silhouette with just enough detail to be satisfying, without overwhelming the page. Best for ages 5 and up.

Dinosaur colors are anyone's guess, which makes this a great creative exercise. Classic green and grey are popular, but orange, red, or blue-grey all make for striking results.
