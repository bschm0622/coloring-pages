The egg has a few decorative sections to color in, but they're large enough that even younger kids can fill them without getting frustrated. It's more structured than the animal pages but still well within reach for ages 4 and up.

This is a good one for practicing color combinations — stripes, polka dots, or a gradient all look great on an Easter egg. Print multiple copies and turn it into a matching game or an Easter decoration.
