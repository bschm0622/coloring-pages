This puppy coloring page uses big, simple shapes — round head, floppy ears, a clear body outline — that make it genuinely easy for young children to color. Many dog coloring pages online have fur shading lines all over them that make the result look messy when filled in with crayon. This one doesn't.

Any color works for an imaginary puppy. Golden yellow, chocolate brown, black and white, or spotted — hand the crayons over and see what they come up with.
