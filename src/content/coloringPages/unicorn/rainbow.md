The rainbow unicorn is basically the ultimate coloring page for kids who love both unicorns and rainbows — which is most kids. The mane and tail are divided into sections that make it obvious where each color goes, so even younger kids can tackle it without getting overwhelmed.

Start with the rainbow sections in the mane and tail — red, orange, yellow, green, blue, purple — then decide on a body color. White keeps it classic, but a light lavender or sky blue body works beautifully too.
