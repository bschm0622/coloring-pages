Penguins are naturally easy to color because of their simple black-and-white shapes — this page leans into that. The outline is clean and uncomplicated, making it accessible for very young kids who just want to fill in the orange beak and feet and call it done.

It's a natural fit for winter-themed crafts or an arctic animals unit. For older kids, try adding a snowy background or experimenting with emperor penguin markings.
